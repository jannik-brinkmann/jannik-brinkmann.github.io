Jannik Brinkmann

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Mannheim. I am interested in understanding the internal mechanisms and representations of neural networks, and using these insights to control and improve them. I am currently a visiting researcher with Prof. He He at the NYU Center for Data Science.

I've also spent the spring and summer of 2024 in Boston, working with the Baulab at Northeastern University. Prior to this, I completed a M.Sc. in Data Science at the University of Mannheim with distinction. I also post about research on LessWrong.
