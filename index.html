Jannik Brinkmann

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, researching the structure of representations and algorithms learned by artificial neural networks and their impact on generalization. I am currently a visiting researcher at the NYU Center for Data Science, working with Prof. He He.

I've also spent the spring and summer of 2024 at Northeastern University, working with Prof. David Bau, with whom I continue to collaborate. Prior to this, I completed a M.Sc. in Data Science at the University of Mannheim.
