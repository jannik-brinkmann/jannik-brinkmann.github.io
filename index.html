about me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, at the University of Mannheim. I am excited about understanding the emergent internal mechanisms of neural networks on complex tasks, such as multi-step reasoning, and using these insights to develop better methods. I completed a M.Sc. in Data Science at the University of Mannheim, and B.Sc. in Computer Science.
