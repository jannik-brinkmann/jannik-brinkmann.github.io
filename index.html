Jannik Brinkmann

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Mannheim. I am interested in understanding the internal mechanisms and representations of neural networks, and using these insights to control and improve them.


I work with the Interpretable Neural Networks lab of Prof. David Bau. I also post about research on LessWrong. Prior to this I completed a M.Sc. in Data Science.
