Jannik Brinkmann

I am interested in the structure of the representations and algorithms learned by artificial neural networks, and their effect on generalisation.

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Mannheim, and a visiting researcher at the NYU Center for Data Science working with Prof. He He. I've also spent the spring and summer of 2024 at Northeastern University working with Prof. David Bau. Prior to this, I completed a M.Sc. in Data Science at the University of Mannheim with distinction.
