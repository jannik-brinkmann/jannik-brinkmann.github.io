about me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, at the University of Mannheim. My research focuses on understanding the internal mechanisms of neural networks, and using these insights to develop better methods. I completed a M.Sc. in Data Science at the University of Mannheim, and a B.Sc. in Computer Science.
